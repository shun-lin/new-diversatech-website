Frequently Asked Questions

more_vert

How is DiversaTech different from other consulting club on campus?

close

We Think Differently. Each of our consulting teams is comprised of individuals from various disciplines across campus from humanities to STEM to bring an interdisciplinary approach to solving challenging industry problems. As much of today’s workforce, especially in tech, relies on cross team collaboration in order to successfully execute and launch a product, we feel it is important to emulate this interdisciplinary structure so the various perspectives on how to tackle a problem are brought to the table. While other consulting organizations are composed of mostly business/economics majors, we seek to channel the many ways Cal prepares students across different majors to analyze and solve problems that are otherwise largely ignored.

more_vert

I’m interested in consulting but I have no experience. Should I apply to DiversaTech?

close

DiversaTech is open to all majors and all backgrounds, including all grade levels (freshmen to seniors). We encourage you to apply! If you do not make it into a consulting team, there are other opportunities like our educational branch to help you gain the skill sets to become a consultant.

more_vert

I am interested in DiversaTech but I may not be able to attend all the meetings. Is that okay?

close

In order to be considered for a consulting team you have to be able to attend all mandatory events. As for info sessions, you just have to attend one. As far as time commitments, meeting times for each consulting time will be different to fit the team's schedule. Also, keep in mind, we have bi-weekly general meetings for all club members.

more_vert

What type of resume is DiversaTech looking for?

close

There is no ideal resume! If not your resume helps us better understand your skills, knowledge, and abilities (KSAs) that are relevant to being a consultant and a team member. So make sure that your resume exemplifies those leadership, team player, and initiative qualities.
